In this challenge you'll start the application and deliberately crash the workflow mid-run, restart it, and prove that completed agent (LLM) calls are replayed from durable state — not re-run. You'll also inspect the workflow's traces in the Aspire dashboard. It will take about 10 minutes to complete.
